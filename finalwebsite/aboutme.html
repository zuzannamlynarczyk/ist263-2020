about me

Hi! I'm Zuzanna and I'm currently a third year undergraduate student studying graphic design at the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University. I'm also part of the iSchool information management & technology minor, as well as the fashion and beauty communication milestone in Newhouse and the School of Visual Performing Arts.
I view everything I do as an art process, taking my time to creatively problem solve—it's all about having fun and feeling the energy of your work! I'm passionate about food, design, fashion, spirituality and art of all sorts. Feel free to reach out with any questions or requests!
