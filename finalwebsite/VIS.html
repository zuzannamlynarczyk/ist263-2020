introduction to typography

design // illustration // typography

VIS 317

Introduction to Typography introduced the basics of layout, grid based design, typography and illustration. I worked on various projects including designing a typeface from scratch, creating USA Forever stamps, a type specimen book and more. This course pushed me to recognize my strengths and weaknesses, as well as how to properly manage my time.

  • role: graphic design, illustration
  • timeline: fall 2020

original typeface

prompt: design an original typeface based on an individual—a muse—and translate their personality into a unique set of characters and symbols.

my idea: to create a modern, but welcoming sans serif typeface for the dean of Newhouse.

type animal illustrations

prompt: illustrate a series of three animals using only the letters in their name.

my idea: to design three arctic animals using different typefacing, but maintaining their cohesion as a group.

typographic history poster

prompt: design a history poster for a signficant event/topic in typographic history.

my idea: to create an informative international typographic style poster that captures the essence of authentic swiss design.
