fashion show posters & invites

The Syracuse Fashion and Design Society (FADS) is an on-campus organization that invites all creatives to join and create fashion related content. We host fashions shows each semster showcasing student clothing design, student set design, student models and more. We also focus on highlighting the importance of supporting sustainable brands and steering cleer of unethical fast fashion.

I work on the graphic design team, creating promotional posters, fliers, and invitations for our events, as well as creative content for our Instagram.

  • role: graphic design, illustration
  • timeline: fall 2019 - present
  • target audience: college students interested in fashion & art
